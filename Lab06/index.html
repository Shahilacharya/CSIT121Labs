The Best Chocolate Cake Ever

  • Recipe by: Shahil Acharya

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, sift dry ingredients together.
  3. Mix in wet ingredients until well combined.
  4. Stir in boiling water. Batter will be thin.
  5. Pour batter evenly into prepared pans.
  6. Bake 30-35 minutes, until toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool in pans 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely.

This chocolate cake is my favorite because it's moist and chocolaty. It's a family recipe passed down for generations.
